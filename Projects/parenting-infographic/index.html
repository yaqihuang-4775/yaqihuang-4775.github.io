Parenting Tips Infographic

INFOGRAPHIC

An infographic translating research-backed parenting advice into a clear, accessible, and parent-friendly visual format.

Overview

This infographic is designed to outline research-based parenting strategies for promoting empathy and preventing aggression in children. The design focused on clarity, comprehensibility, and real-life relatability so that parents could quickly understand and apply the recommendations.
