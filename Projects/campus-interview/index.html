Mood Psychology Campus Interview

VIDEO / INTERVIEW PROJECT

A student-centered campus interview project involving planning, writing, coordination, on-site participation, and communication-driven storytelling.

Overview

This project involved planning and recording a short campus interview video designed to capture student voices in an engaging and relatable format. The visual direction focused on natural conversation, clear pacing, and a friendly campus atmosphere so that the final piece would feel approachable, authentic, and easy to watch.
