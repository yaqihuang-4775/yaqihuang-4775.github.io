YSRA Online Tools

INSTAGRAM POST SERIES

A social media carousel series introducing useful online tools through structured information design and platform-conscious visuals.

Overview

This Instagram post series is designed for a non-profit organization, Youth & Students Resources Association (YSRA), for the purpose of sharing information with students about online tools useful for presentation creation. The design of the post focused on content organization and visual layout to clearly showcase and emphasize the information.
