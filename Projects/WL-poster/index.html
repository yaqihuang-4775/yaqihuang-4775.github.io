World Languages Club Posters

POSTER SERIES

A poster series designed to promote campus events through engaging layouts, strong hierarchy, and cohesive visual identity.

Overview

This series of posters are created to encourage participation in various language tutoring opportunities hosted by a school club. The design focused on attractiveness, clarity, and content organization to outline the requirements, benefits, and application portal for the activities and programs.
